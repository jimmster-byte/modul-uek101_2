What is Project Nintendo?

Project Nintendo is my personal school project. I’ve created this site because I grew up with Nintendo Systems and spent most of my free time playing them. Basically this site is just an information page on the various Nintendo consoles.

Why this Project?

I’ve spent most of my time watching my brother play Super Mario World 3 since I didn’t know how to play the game. He was a natural with the controller and cleared most of the levels at a young age. It blew my mind. Despite the gameplay, I was amazed by everything: the character sprites, the music, the level design and the game mechanics. It was a big deal for me back then. So since that day on, I’ve become a big fan.
